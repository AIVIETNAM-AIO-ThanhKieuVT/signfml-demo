SignFML

Latent Flow Matching for Gloss-Free Sign Language Production

CVPR 2026 Submission 10x Fewer NFEs

Abstract

We present SignFML, a novel framework that formulates text-to-sign generation as conditional latent flow matching. By leveraging a structured latent space and a multi-scale flow block, SignFML produces high-fidelity sign language poses with significantly better temporal consistency and lower computational cost than diffusion-based methods.
